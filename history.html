How did it all start?

It all started very differently. The action took place not in Poland 2023, but in Japan 2014. The main character was Takeshi Miyazaki, not the familiar Andrey Yurkevich. The game's goal was to befriend M.C.'s younger brother, Jin, and take care of him. You might be wondering, "Why don't Takeshi and Jin's parents take care of them?" The answer might be disappointing, as they don't have parents. More specifically, their father left when their second child was born, and their mother died of leukemia (blood cancer). Takeshi is forced to work as a courier to support himself and his younger brother.
During development, the original plot struck me as too harsh. Essentially, these were children trying to survive in a world that was very cruel to them.
I decided to abandon this idea and rework the plot, and essentially the entire game. Although the meaning remained more or less harsh, it became less prickly and was more focused on the main character.
